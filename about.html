About the Project

Marina Cone

Marina Cone is the lead researcher on the NZ Digital Literary Heritage Project. Her work focuses on digital preservation, archival storytelling, and experimental literature across media platforms.

Professor David Ciccoricco

Dave Ciccoricco is Professor of English at the University of Otago, and is leading the DHLP project.

Dark – Jin-Shou Tang

Dark, by Taiwanese artist and macabre puppeteer Jin-Shou Tang, displays his repertoire of disturbing persons and personalities in a clickable microcosm of social dystopia. The work plays out a bit like an Internet hotel at the very end of the information highway, in which every room harbours a lost soul of some sort. We are invited to pass through rooms and scenes which react to the click of a mouse in unusual and often unexpected ways.

Part of the NZDLHPP collection.

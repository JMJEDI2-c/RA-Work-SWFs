About the Work

Title:Dark

Author:Jin-Shou Tang

Description: This Flash-based literary artifact was preserved through the New Zealand Digital Literary Heritage Project. Taiwanese artist and macabre puppeteer Jin-Shou Tang displays his repertoire of disturbing persons and personalities in his clickable microcosm of social dystopia; Dark. The work plays out a bit like an Internet hotel at the very end of the information highway, in which every room harbours a lost soul of some sort. We are invited to pass through rooms and scenes which react to the click of a mouse in unusual and often unexpected ways.

About the Work

Title: Capital

Author: Ian Finch

Description: This Flash-based interactive literary work was preserved through the New Zealand Digital Literary Heritage Project. "Capital" is an examination of various streets in Wellington via poetry. By clicking on a street and then mousing over various points on that street, lines in the poem appear.
